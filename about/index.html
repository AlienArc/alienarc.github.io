About Alien Arc

Alien Arc Technologies, LLC is a modern applications company specializing in Mobile, Web, Desktop, and IoT solutions built primarily on the .NET stack. Founded in 2014, Alien Arc partners with organizations across a wide range of industries to design, modernize, and deliver reliable software solutions, with a strong emphasis on cross-platform development using .NET MAUI and Blazor.

Alien Arc delivers high-quality user experiences across mobile, web, and desktop platforms by leveraging modern architectures and cloud services to automate processes, accelerate delivery, and ensure long-term maintainability.

Alien Arc has delivered solutions in industries including fitness and membership services, marine and industrial manufacturing, professional coaching, staffing and workforce management, and distribution. Engagements frequently involve rescuing or modernizing legacy systems, strengthening architectural foundations, and implementing automated deployment pipelines to reduce operational risk and improve development velocity.

Representative work includes:

  • Building cross-platform mobile applications for iOS and Android using Xamarin Forms and later .NET MAUI and MAUI Blazor Hybrid, supporting offline data synchronization, time tracking, approvals, and workforce coordination.
  • Developing Blazor-based web applications and dashboards for internal teams and external customers, enabling job management, reporting, scheduling, and operational visibility.
  • Delivering WPF and embedded systems for marine battery management solutions, including real-time monitoring, CAN bus communication, firmware and configuration updates, diagnostics, and kiosk-style user interfaces deployed in harsh maritime environments.
  • Creating multi-tenant, white-labeled web platforms for professional services organizations, featuring custom business logic engines, PDF reporting, and mobile-friendly user experiences.
  • Implementing Azure-based backend services, including Azure Functions, to support mobile and web applications.
  • Developing custom EDI and ERP integration solutions, automating order intake, exception handling, invoicing, and fulfillment workflows.

In addition to client delivery, Alien Arc Technologies is deeply invested in the .NET developer community. The company actively supports and leads the Kansas City .NET User Group and regularly presents at developer conferences including KCDC, DevUp, Nebraska.Code(), TechBash, and Build Stuff. Team members are recognized community experts, including a Microsoft MVP, who regularly speak on topics such as .NET MAUI, Blazor, unit testing, DevOps, and related technologies.

Alien Arc Technologies provides development and consulting services for organizations seeking to build or modernize iOS, Android, Web, Desktop, or IoT solutions using modern .NET technologies.
