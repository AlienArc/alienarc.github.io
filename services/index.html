Services

Custom Software Development Link to heading

Alien Arc Technologies designs and delivers custom software solutions tailored to the unique needs of each business. We work closely with our clients to understand their workflows, constraints, and goals, then build software that fits—rather than forcing the business to adapt to off-the-shelf tools.

Our team specializes in modern .NET-based applications across web, desktop, and embedded environments. Whether building a new system from the ground up or modernizing an existing solution, we focus on clean architecture, maintainability, and long-term value so our clients can confidently evolve their software as their business grows.

Project Jumpstart & Team Acceleration Link to heading

Alien Arc Technologies helps teams get projects moving quickly and confidently. Whether you’re starting a new initiative, inheriting an existing codebase, or struggling to gain momentum, we can step in to jumpstart development and establish a strong technical foundation.

We work directly alongside your team to clarify requirements, define architecture, and quickly deliver working software. Our jumpstart engagements often include hands-on development, architectural guidance, and best-practice setup for tooling, testing, and deployment. The goal is to reduce uncertainty, accelerate progress, and leave your team in a stronger position to continue building long after the engagement ends.

Mobile & Cross-Platform Solutions Link to heading

We help organizations reach users wherever they are by building high-quality iOS and Android applications using modern cross-platform technologies like .NET MAUI and MAUI Blazor Hybrid. This approach allows us to deliver native experiences while maximizing code reuse and reducing long-term maintenance costs.

Alien Arc has deep experience building mobile solutions that support real-world requirements such as offline operation, synchronization, device integration, and secure backend communication. From workforce and field-service applications to customer-facing mobile products, we focus on performance, usability, and reliability.

Cloud, Integration & Application Modernization Link to heading

Many of our engagements focus on modernizing existing systems and improving how applications integrate with one another. Alien Arc helps organizations move away from fragile, manual processes by introducing cloud-based services, automation, and modern deployment pipelines.

We design and implement backend services using Microsoft Azure, including APIs, Azure Functions, and integrations with third-party systems such as ERPs and EDI platforms. Our modernization work often reduces operational risk, improves scalability, and enables teams to deliver features faster with greater confidence.

Training & Advisory Services Link to heading

In addition to development, Alien Arc provides training and advisory services to help teams adopt modern .NET technologies and development practices. Our consultants bring real-world experience building production systems and can guide teams through architecture decisions, tooling choices, and best practices.

We can deliver workshops, mentoring, and technical guidance on topics such as .NET MAUI, Blazor, application architecture, unit testing, and DevOps. Alien Arc is also deeply involved in the developer community, leading user groups and speaking at conferences—experience we bring directly into our client engagements.
